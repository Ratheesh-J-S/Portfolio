Project information

  • Title: Desert Night Tours
  • Project site: www.desertnighttours.com

The Desert Night Tours Admin Panel is a comprehensive and dynamic web-based management system designed to streamline administrative operations for a full-service tour and travel business. Built with HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and Bootstrap 5 on the frontend and powered by Laravel on the backend, the platform offers an intuitive and scalable environment for managing a wide range of business components. It enables administrators to efficiently oversee tour categories, yacht cruise options, attraction ticket listings, and a variety of vehicle categories including cars, buses, and vans.

The system also supports the management of destinations, adventure activities, New Year Eve packages, visa types, and blog content. Featuring a powerful switch-based control mechanism, admins can seamlessly create, edit, publish, and delete listings. The panel includes advanced pricing calculation methods, a secure cloud-based image storage solution, and robust user role management with account activation/deactivation, OTP-based password recovery, and activity tracking. It facilitates bookings across all services—tours, events, cruises, vehicles, tickets, and visas—and automates customer communications via email.

Additionally, it incorporates a multi-category rating system for collecting customer feedback on all services, and generates detailed user reports for every package, rental, and visa application. The platform also includes complete company profile management and prioritizes security while offering a clean and user-friendly interface. Designed to support growth and improve customer engagement, the Desert Night Tours Admin Panel is a vital tool for operational efficiency and service excellence in the travel industry.
